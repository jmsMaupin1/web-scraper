About Kenzie Academy

Kenzie Academy is a campus-based and online school that is designed and taught by industry practitioners. It is a unique community that equips you with necessary skills and support to launch your career in tech.

Admissions Criteria

We use our Kenzie Skill Check to help determine admission. We look for people who are self-motivated and want a brighter future for themselves. We test for critical thinking skills, technical aptitude, and a good attitude. We DO NOT look at test scores or transcripts. Learn More >>

Program Cost

We offer three ways to finance your tuition including an Income Share Agreement, monthly payment plans, and the option to pay tuition upfront. Learn More >>

Programs Available Online or On-Campus

Attend at our Indianapolis campus or online from the comfort of your home. Students have access to instructors and coaches in person, over video conferencing and on Slack.
